about me

Hey! I'm Joseph, a highschool sophomore game developer! I'm from Maryland and I'm working on "Mind Garden," my hand-drawn story game!

When I'm not coding or drawing, I'm exploring national parks with my dog or working on my YouTube channel where I talk about games like Animal Crossing and Zelda.

Currently a student at Calvert Hall College High School (class of 2027!) with a 4.0 weighted GPA.

my projects

main project

Mind Garden

An adventure game where players explore a serene garden environment, make friends, uncover mysteries, and create vibrant memories. Features a unique blend of hand-drawn crayon art and real-life photos.

Players receive a journal to document their journey, collecting scraps, stickers, and treasures to personalize their experience. Developed with the Godot engine.
